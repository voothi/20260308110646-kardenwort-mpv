## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: High-Contrast Active Indicator
The Drum Window SHALL use a high-contrast color for the active subtitle line to ensure it is clearly distinguishable from the background and inactive lines.

#### Scenario: Viewing active line
- **WHEN** the Drum Window is active
- **THEN** the line corresponding to the current playback time SHALL be rendered in High-Contrast White (`FFFFFF`).
