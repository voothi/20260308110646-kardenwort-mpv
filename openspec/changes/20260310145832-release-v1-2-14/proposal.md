## Why

This change formalizes the Terminology Standardization & Goal Refinement introduced in Release v1.2.14. The project's identity has evolved from a "Language Learning" tool to a comprehensive **Language Acquisition Suite**. This update standardizes the project's "dictionary" to align with the philosophy of extensive acquisition through immersion, emphasizing the convenient consumption of dual-subtitle material.

## What Changes

- Comprehensive audit and update of the project's terminology: "Language Learning" is replaced with **"Language Acquisition"**, and "Study" is replaced with **"Immersion"** or **"Acquisition"**.
- Refinement of project goals in `README.md` to prioritize the **convenient consumption** of Dual-Subtitle content (DualSubs) in local offline workflows.
- Implementation of a clear workflow distinction between pre-processing (external preparation) and playback (immersion using this suite).
- Formal documentation of the suite's role in mitigating context loss when acquiring language from lower-quality sources like YouTube auto-captions.

## Capabilities

### New Capabilities
- `language-acquisition-standardization`: A project-wide terminology standard that promotes the philosophy of acquisition through immersion.
- `consumption-focused-documentation`: Documentation that prioritizes the user experience of content consumption over technical data transformation.

### Modified Capabilities
- None (Philosophical and documentation pivot).

## Impact

- **Conceptual Clarity**: A more accurate description of the suite's role in the language acquisition process.
- **Improved Discoverability**: Refined goals make it easier for users to understand how the suite fits into their immersion workflow.
- **Professionalism**: Standardized terminology across all metadata, scripts, and reports.
