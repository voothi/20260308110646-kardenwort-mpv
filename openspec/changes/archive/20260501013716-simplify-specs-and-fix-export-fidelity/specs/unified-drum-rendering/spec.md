## MODIFIED Requirements

### Requirement: Highlighting Stack (Req 8)
**UPDATED**: The `calculate_highlight_stack` and `format_highlighted_word` utilities SHALL ensure that word-level highlighting is strictly bound to word-character tokens.

### Requirement: Consistent Punctuation Coloring (Req 13)
**REMOVED**: The requirement to color brackets and punctuation consistently is DEPRECATED in favor of the Surgical Model.
